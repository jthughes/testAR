Museum AR

This website was an experiment in making a way of productively introducing Augmented Reality (AR) technology into the classroom.

There are essentially two ingredients: 3D scans of items from museum collections freely provided by museums, and the ar.js js library to provide the mechanism for using the models.

The idea is to print off (or display on screen) a QR code linking to a webpage containing a ar.js view. The QR code can then be scanned on a phone and will render the 3D model above the QR code.
